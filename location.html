Location

2174 Market Street
San Francisco, CA 94114

Swedish American Hall

www.swedishamericanhall.com

Public Transportation

Café Du Nord and the Swedish American Hall are located on the north side of Market Street, between Church Street and Sanchez Street. We are easily accessible by public transportation. The F-Line streetcar stops at the corner of Sanchez and Market. Church and Market is also a major Muni stop; the N, M, L and J lines all stop at the Church Street Station.

Parking

Street parking in the neighborhood is generally available, but can be limited.

From The East Bay

Once off the Bay Bridge, stay on 101 and proceed toward the Golden Gate Bridge. Take the Mission Street exit and go straight through the first stop light. The street ends at Market Street. Turn left on Market, and Café Du Nord will be on the right side of the street at the end of the block one block past Safeway.

From The South Bay

Take 101 North to the Mission Street exit. Go straight through the first stop light. The street ends at Market Street. Turn left on Market, and Café Du Nord will be on the right side of the street at the end of the block one block past Safeway.

Conference Team

Golden Gate Ruby Conference is a production of Golden Gate Ruby Conference, LLC. Your conference organizers are:

Josh Susser

Josh likes to say that Ruby is the love child of Lisp and Smalltalk, raised by Perl the eccentric nanny. At Pivotal Labs he spends most of his time developing Rails web applications for clients, and he is a frequent contributor to the Rails open source project. He has spoken at RailsConf, regional conferences and meetups. You can find his blog at blog.hasmanythrough.com.

Leah Silber

If you've been to a Ruby conference in the last year or two, you've likely seen Leah there. When she's not traveling the world seeking out the perfect conference, she's here in San Francisco running Marketing and Community Relations at Engine Yard.
